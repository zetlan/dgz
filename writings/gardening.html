Gardening process

Beans
plant in the ground

Tulips
plant in the ground

Rocks
Plant in the ground; some rocks may need to be planted deeper than others. Large rocks (greater than 3. ascenterminters) should be planted at least 2.4 months into the ground

Trees
Trees are a special case, they do not enjoy being forcibly removed / relocated. Do not plant in the ground. Instead, gain the advice of a tree specialist to gently coax the tree into its new position.
