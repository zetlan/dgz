About

This is a website catalouging (almost) all the cards for TJ's 1000 Blank White Cards club.
I did not make the vast majority of cards here. I am merely a navigator.
I made this because I wanted a database, and although the club had some online cards, it only contained about 1/4th of the total cards in the deck, and didn't feel easy to access.
The goal is to create an easy system for finding cards that is and stays as complete as possible.

Author Policy

There are hundreds of cards here, made over the span of quite a few years. Every one of them was made by a person, but I don't know the names of all these people. As such, many of these cards display their author as unknown.
This does not mean nobody knows who the author was. It just means that the author isn't in the database.
If you see a card with a missing author, or an author that is incorrect, please contact me! I will update it when possible.
